# Privacy Policy - Guitar Trainer Pro

**Last updated: December 28, 2024**

## Overview
Guitar Trainer Pro ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our data practices.

## Data Collection
**We do not collect any personal data.**

The App:
- Does NOT collect personal information
- Does NOT track your usage
- Does NOT use analytics or tracking services
- Does NOT share data with third parties
- Does NOT require account creation

## Permissions

### Microphone Access
The App requests microphone permission to:
- Detect the musical notes you play on your guitar
- Provide real-time audio feedback

**Important:**
- Audio is processed locally on your device only
- No audio is recorded, saved, or transmitted
- Audio processing stops when you close the app or disable the microphone

### Data Storage
The App may store:
- Your app settings (locally on your device only)
- These settings never leave your device

## Third-Party Services
The App does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is safe for all ages.
